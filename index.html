About Me

A dedicated and creative fourth-year student in CSC2463 (Interactive Media) with a passion for exploring the intersection of code, sound, and visual design. This portfolio showcases a collection of projects developed using p5.js and Tone.js, demonstrating skills in interactive programming, audio synthesis, and creative problem-solving.

Throughout these assignments, I've explored various concepts in interactive media, from basic graphics and sound manipulation to more complex interactive installations. The final project, Synth Runner: An Immersive Audio-Visual Experience, highlights my ability to integrate physical interfaces (Arduino) with web-based technologies to create engaging user experiences.
